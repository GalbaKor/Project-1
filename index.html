About

We are a non profit organisation operating a Pay As You Feel restaurant, café and warehouse.
Working alongside local distributors, food that would otherwise be sent to a landfill is instead diverted to us.
We then create meals with said food in our restaurant and café, or give to customers via our warehouse subscription service.

What is food waste?

Food waste is just expired and leftover food. WRONG.
Food waste isn't just the stuff that we throw away after a meal. It can also be:

  • Surplus that isn't required by a restaurant or supermarket

  • Food that reached its best before date but is still perfectly fine and edible

  • Food that doesn't reach the strict cosmetic standards held by retailers.
    A misshapen or large apple is still an apple

  • Many fish (up to 60%!) are discarded simply because they are the wrong size or species

Enter House of Flavour!

Encouraged by other PAYF (Pay As You Feel) shops and Non-Profit Organisations, we decided to give our own take on a restaurant and café with a changing daily menu using healthy and edible food that would otherwise be destined for the landfill...

What's more, we have started a service whereby patrons can subscribe below to collect an assortment of fresh fruit, veg and cupboard goods from our warehouse on site. Perfect for the amateur cooks out there!

How can you help the food waste crisis?

We are actively seeking volunteers for roles such as cooking and waitering at the restaurant and café and management of goods via our warehouse.

If you wish to aid our fight against food waste, get in contact through our form below. If you can't volunteer, don't worry! You are still helping by attending our restaurant and café or subscribing to our warehouse collection scheme.
